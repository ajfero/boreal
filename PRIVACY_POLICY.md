# Privacy Policy

This extension, **Boreal**, is an internal company tool developed for internal use.

## Data Collection

This extension does not collect, track, or share any personal user data. All processing occurs locally within the user's browser, and no data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.

## Data Storage

Any configuration or user preferences are stored exclusively in the browser's local storage (via the `chrome.storage` API) and remain on the user's device.

## Contact

For any questions regarding this policy, please contact your internal IT department.
